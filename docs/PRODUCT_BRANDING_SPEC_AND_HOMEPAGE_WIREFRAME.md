# DIGIGO E-SOFT Product Branding Spec

## 1. Brand Positioning
- Product Name: `DIGIGO E-SOFT`
- Core Promise: `Stop hard water damage without salt, chemicals, or water waste.`
- Positioning Statement: `Made in India, long-life, no-consumable electronic water conditioning for homes, businesses, factories, and farms.`

## 2. Brand Pillars
- Made in India Engineering
- 30+ Year Design Life
- Zero Consumables
- Visible Scale-Reduction Impact

## 3. Visual System
- Primary Color: Deep Blue (`#0D4D91`)
- Secondary Color: Signal Cyan (`#00A7C7`)
- Accent Color: Saffron (`#FF9933`)
- Trust Accent: India Green (`#138808`)
- Background Family: Clean cool whites and light blue surfaces
- Icon Style: Line icons, medium stroke, rounded corners
- Motif: Signal-wave gradients and circular badge cues

## 4. Typography
- Display: `Sora`
- Body/UI: `Manrope`
- Headline Behavior: Bold, concise, outcome-led
- Body Behavior: Plain language, short paragraphs, no jargon blocks

## 5. Messaging Rules
- Always refer to product as `DIGIGO E-SOFT`
- Use outcome phrases:
  - `No salt handling`
  - `No backwash waste`
  - `Long design life`
  - `Clear maintenance value`
- Avoid deep technical jargon on first fold

## 6. Proof Framework
- Numeric proof: installation count, segment footprint, retention
- Logo proof: client wall visible on homepage
- Testimonial proof: short, impact-focused, segment-tagged quotes
- Comparison proof: E-SOFT vs Salt Softener table

## 7. CTA System
- Primary CTA: `Get Technical Fit`
- Secondary CTA: `View Working Principle`
- Support CTA: `Talk to Product Team`
